What it is

Peruvian Nights is a small-group, private cooking experience. We come to you with everything needed for an unforgettable evening: fresh ingredients, tools, and a curated soundtrack. You’ll learn to craft iconic Peruvian flavors and make classic pisco cocktails.

  • Groups of 6–12
  • Host-at-home or private venue
  • 2 cocktails included (Pisco Sour, Chilcano)
  • Hands-on appetizer + main dish

Quick facts

  • Duration: 2.5–3 hours
  • Service area: DMV (DC, MD, VA)
  • Pricing: from $85 per person (min 6)
  • Add‑ons: dessert, photography, extra cocktails
